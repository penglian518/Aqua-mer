Step 2. Searching Parameters
This step provides three algorithms to perform conformation search. Each algorithm requires different parameters. You will be asked to provide them accordingly.

1) Random search. Generate N conformations by randomly rotating the single bonds and than minimize.

2) Replica exchange. Perform an MD simulation with replica exchange technique to search the conformations in gas phase, implicit solvent and explicit solvent models.

3) Random DFT. Similar to 1) method but will minimize with DFT methods. Suitable for compound with metal atoms
