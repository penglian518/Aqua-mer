a) Random samplingThis method will first generate the configurations by randomly assigning the dihedral angles in the molecules, then optimize with generalized forcefields, such as UFF, GAFF, MMFF etc.
b) Random sampling with DFT optimizerSimilar to the Random sampling method, but will optimize with semi-empirical quantum chemistry methods. This method provides better evaluation of molecules with heavy metals, but it's time consuming. We suggest to use the Random sampling method first.
c) Replica exchange samplingThis module allows the use to perform conformation search of small compounds in explicit water, implicit water and gas phase with replica exchange algorithm implemented in NAMD2. Antechamber is used to parametrize molecules.
