a) Random sampling with MM optimizationThis method will first generate the configurations by randomly assigning the dihedral angles in the molecules, then optimize with a generalized force field, such as UFF, GAFF, or MMFF.
b) Random sampling with QM optimizationSimilar to the Random sampling with MM optimization method. Instead of optimizing the molecules with a force field, this method will optimize them with semi-empirical quantum chemistry methods. This method provides a better evaluation of molecules with heavy metals, but it is time-consuming. We suggest using random sampling with MM optimization first.
c) MD simulation with replica exchange samplingThis module allows the user to perform a conformational search of small compounds in explicit water, implicit water and gas phase with replica exchange algorithm implemented in NAMD2. Antechamber is used to parameterize molecules.
