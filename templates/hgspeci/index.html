Instructions
Steps for Hg speciation:

Step 1. Input parameters.
a. Input physical conditions (units, pH range, pe, density, temperatures, redox ...)
b. Input master species (provide those in database, and the option to add your own data. If add your own data, provide the option to add the "Solution Species" and "Phases")

Step 2. Database parameters display and modification. Just display the related data (according to the input parameters from Step 1. Provide options for modify data, including add and remove the related reactions/data

Step 3. Review & submit. Before submission, you can review your input parameters and parameters from database.

Step 4. Download the results. Once the job is finished, you can view your results and download them.

Notice: Once the compound is uploaded, a unique job ID will be assigned. With the job ID, you can check your results and redo analysis quickly.
