Toolkit for theoretical Chemistry calculation

under construction

This toolkit is designed to provide a user-friendly interface for users with or without a theoretical chemistry background to calculate missing parameters required for their particular speciation problem, such as association constants (log K). As listed bellow, ther are four independent tools. The users may use them separately or in combination.

a) Conformational search
b) Solvation free energy (ΔGsolv) calculation
c) Proton dissociation constant (pKa) calculation
d) Association constant (log K) calculation
