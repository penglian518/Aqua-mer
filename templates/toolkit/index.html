Toolkit for theoretical Chemistry calculation

under construction

This toolkit is designed to provide an user-friendly interface for users with/without theoretical Chemistry background to calculate the parameters required by speciation studies, such as association constants (log K). As listed bellow, it provides four independent tools. The users are encouraged to use them separately or as a combine.

a) Conformational search
b) Solvation free energy (ΔGsolv) calculation
c) Proton dissociation constant (pKa) calculation
d) Association constant (log K) calculation
