Toolkit for theoretical chemistry calculation

This toolkit is designed to provide a user-friendly interface for users with or without a theoretical chemistry background to calculate missing parameters required for their particular speciation problem, such as stability constants (log K). As listed bellow, there are four independent modules. The users may use them separately or in a combination.

Independent modules

a) Conformational search
b) Solvation free energy (ΔGsolv) calculation
c) Proton dissociation constant (pKa) calculation
d) Stability constant (log K) calculation
